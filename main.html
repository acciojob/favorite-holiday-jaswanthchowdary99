Visakhapatnam


It is called the city of destiny, It is very beautiful city with very heart welcoming people. It has some very good beaches and many more sight seeing locations.

  1. RK Beach - A popular beach known for its scenic views.
  2. Kailasagiri - A hilltop park with stunning views of the city and sea.
  3. Bheemili Beach - A quieter and less crowded beach with historical significance.
  4. Rushikonda Beach - Famous for water sports and golden sands.
  5. Thotlakonda - An ancient Buddhist site on a hill with panoramic views.
  • Noodles - A popular street food with local flavors.
  • Laddu Gopal Chat - A famous chat that is both sweet and spicy.
  • Biriyani - A must-try aromatic rice dish.
  • Seafood - Freshly caught and cooked to perfection.
  • Ice Cream - Perfect to cool down after a day at the beach.
